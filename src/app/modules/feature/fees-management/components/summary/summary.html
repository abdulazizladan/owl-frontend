School Fees Management Module Features

  • Fee Structure Definition:
    • Ability to define different fee types (tuition, hostel, PTA levy, development levy, examination fees, uniforms, extracurriculars, etc.).
    • Set fees per term/session, per class, or per student category (e.g., new students vs. returning students).
    • Option for sibling discounts.
    • Ability to define payment plans (e.g., installment options).
  • Fee Collection & Payment Tracking:
    • Record payments made (cash, bank transfer, POS, online payment gateway).
    • Generate payment receipts (printable and digital).
    • Track outstanding balances per student.
    • Automated calculation of pro-rata fees for students joining mid-term.
  • Online Payment Integration:
    • Integrate with popular Nigerian payment gateways (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave, Remita) to allow parents to pay online.
    • Automatic reconciliation of online payments with student accounts.
  • Reporting & Analytics:
    • Generate reports on total fees collected, outstanding fees, fees by class/term, fees by payment method.
    • List of debtors/defaulters.
    • Financial summaries for management.
    • Export data to Excel/PDF.
  • Invoicing & Billing:
    • Generate automated invoices for each student per term/session.
    • Include breakdown of fee components.
    • Option to send invoices via email or SMS.
  • Reminders & Notifications:
    • Automated SMS/email reminders for upcoming fee deadlines.
    • Notifications for overdue payments to parents/guardians.
  • Waiver & Concession Management:
    • Ability to apply fee waivers or concessions for specific students.
    • Track and approve waiver requests.
  • Refund Management:
    • Process and track fee refunds (e.g., for withdrawn students).
  • Transaction History:
    • Comprehensive ledger for each student showing all fee charges, payments, and outstanding balances.
  • User Roles & Permissions:
    • Define roles for Bursar, Accountant, Administrator with specific access rights to fee management functions.
